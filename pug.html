Pug

●The pug is a breed of DOG with physically distinctive features of a wrinkly, short-muzzled face, and curled tail.

●Weight: 14-18 pounds

●Group: Toy Group


■COLOUR: ●The breed has a fine, glossy coat that comes in a variety of colours, most often fawn or black.

■BODY: ●Pug has a compact square body with well-developed muscles.

■NATURE: ●Pugs are known for being sociable and gentle COMPANION DOG The AMERICAN KENNEL CLUB describes the breed's personality as "even-tempered and charming"

Characteristics

■Physical characteristics: Their smooth and glossy coats can be fawn, apricot fawn, silver fawn, or black.

●modern breed preferences are for a square COBBY body, a compact form, a deep chest, and well-developed muscle.

●Their smooth and glossy coats can be fawn, apricot fawn, silver fawn, or black.

■Temperament: ●This breed is often described by the Latin phrase multum in parvo, or "much in little" or "a lot of dog in a small space",

● Alluding to the Pug's remarkable and charming personality, despite its small size Pugs are strong willed but rarely aggressive, and are suitable for families with children.

●The majority of the breed is very fond of children and sturdy enough to properly play with them.

●Pugs are playful and thrive on human companionship.They also tend to have a snoozy nature and spend a lot of time napping.

Additionally


■Birth and reproduction: ●Pug pregnancies last an average of 63 days. The average size litter for Pugs is 4 to 6 puppies, though this can vary from 1 to 9 and still be considered normal.

■Chinese origins: ●CHINA is the earliest known source for the breed.


Food Habits

●There are several vegetables your Pug will love such as carrots, green beans, sweet potatoes etc.

●The best part is that several of them will be beneficial for their health.

●The dark green vegetables such as spinach, broccoli, kale, and etc contain vitamins and minerals that will have a positive effect on your Pugs health.

●Inherited disorders

●A Pug's normal body temperature is between 101 °F (38 °C) and 102 °F (39 °C). If this temperature rises to 105 °F (41 °C), oxygen demand is greatly increased and immediate cooling is required. If body temperature reaches 108 °F (42 °C), organ failure can occur.

●Pugs have many WRINKLE in their faces, so owners will often clean inside the creases to avoid irritation and infection.

■TIME SPAN: ●Life Expectancy: 13-15 years
